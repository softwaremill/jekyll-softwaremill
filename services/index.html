How we work

We believe in small, focused teams. That means:

    • a well-organized team that knows what to do from the day one
    • people with great communication skills and highest business culture standards
    • full transparency of project progress
    • best value for money

Usually a team consists of 2-4 people responsible for all stages of software development, from business analysis to deployment.

They are led by ScrumMaster and supported by a web/UX designer or QA engineer depending on the needs.
